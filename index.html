    Rules
    1. Must be a Xylz-dedicated challenge.
    2. Good gameplay and decoration needed. No layouts.
    3. Challenge must be short or tiny, not exceeding 30 seconds.
    4. Requires legitimate verification with click proof.
    5. Max 15 CPS, not spam based (flexibly defined).
    6. No microclicks or swift-clicking.
    7. No entirely invisible levels to prevent noclipping or post-verification buffs.
    8. No minigames.
    9. Duplicates allowed if you're the original creator or have their consent and the original level must be a Xylz challenge.
    10. If gameplay resembles an existing challenge, the harder one is kept. Permission required if using existing gameplay.
    11. Buffing or remaking challenges requires original creator's permission.
    12. Certain hacks permitted (FPS Bypass 50-360, Icon Hack, RGB Icons). No FPS abuse, macros, NoClip/NoSpike, Speedhacks, Semi-Auto, secretly nerfing levels, refresh rate impossibilities, or cutting completions.
    If you have any questions, or you think a level is unfairly placed, please send your concerns to the Discord.
